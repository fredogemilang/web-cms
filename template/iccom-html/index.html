Who Are We?

iCCom (Indonesia Cloud Community) is a welcoming space for anyone who's passionate about cloud technology and its development.

We're a nonprofit community dedicated to growing cloud talent and expertise across Indonesia through shared learning and support.

Whether you're just getting started or already experienced, if cloud tech excites you, you'll fit right in.

iCCom Core Value

Embrace Learning

People learn together, trade ideas, back each other up, and grow side by side.

Nurture Growth

Everyone keeps improving at their own pace while helping the community rise with them.

Strategic Alliance

Friendships and collaborations form naturally here, creating opportunities everyone can be part of.
